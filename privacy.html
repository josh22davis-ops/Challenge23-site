Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 10, 2026

Overview

Challenge 23 ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you sign in with Apple, we may receive:

  • Your name (if you choose to share it)
  • Your email address (or Apple's private relay email)
  • A unique user identifier

Health Data

With your permission, we access:

  • Heart rate data during workouts
  • Resting heart rate measurements
  • Workout session data

Health data is stored locally on your device and optionally synced to your private account via secure cloud storage.

Bluetooth Data

We connect to Bluetooth heart rate monitors to receive real-time heart rate data during workouts. We do not collect or store information about your Bluetooth devices.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide workout tracking and heart rate monitoring features
  • To calculate training quality scores and recovery metrics
  • To sync your data across your devices
  • To enable group challenges with friends (if you choose to join)

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored using Firebase (Google Cloud), which employs industry-standard encryption and security practices. Health data is encrypted both in transit and at rest.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share data:

  • With group members if you join a challenge group (only workout summaries you choose to share)
  • With service providers (Firebase/Google) who help operate our app
  • If required by law

Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time
  • Revoke HealthKit permissions in iOS Settings
  • Request a copy of your data

Children's Privacy

Challenge 23 is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app.
